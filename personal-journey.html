Sheila’s Story: Real, Raw, Resilient

Hey there. I’m Sheila Marie De Guzman — or just Sheila when the world gets too much noise. If you’re here, you probably want to know the real me, not the polished, corporate-approved version.

So, buckle up — here’s my journey, no fluff, just the facts, the feels, and a whole lot of fight.

The Degree That Got Away

I started out in Hospitality Management because I love people, details, and making things just right. But life? Life had other plans. Circumstances, struggles, and some heavy personal storms made finishing that degree impossible.

It’s tough. It’s frustrating. But I’m not broken — just paused. And honestly? The real world taught me lessons no classroom ever could.

The Hustle Never Stops

Despite not having that shiny diploma, I dove headfirst into the working world — customer support, account coordination, tech troubleshooting, you name it.

I mastered the tools, sharpened my people skills, and learned to handle everything from cranky customers to chaotic backends. Every tough day was a step toward resilience.

The Job Hunt Chronicles

Yes, I’m still searching. Not because I lack skills, but because I’m holding out for the right fit — a role where I can thrive, grow, and be appreciated.

This market? It’s wild. But I’m not here to settle. If you want someone who shows up, learns fast, and brings heart and hustle? That’s me.

Hazure Vale: My Other Side

When I’m not troubleshooting or coordinating, I write. Under the name Hazure Vale, I explore stories that question identity, rebellion, and survival — all wrapped up in my Room Zer0 series.

Writing keeps me sane. It’s my rebellion, my therapy, my passion.

What I Bring To The Table

Real talk? I bring grit, empathy, and unshakable persistence. I know what it’s like to face setbacks and still stand tall.

If you want someone who’s authentic, hardworking, and ready to get shit done — I’m your girl.

Thanks for reading. Now, let’s make some magic happen.
